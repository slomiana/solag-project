" We are passionate about empowering and transforming the lives of children and young people in The Gambia through education and sports."

Our Aim:

  • To play a quota in national development through the provision and support of educational and sporting activities through service delivery and advocacy. This will be supported by the following three underlining principles:
  • A commitment to preparing children and young people for participation
  • Promoting acceptance and understanding of others
  • Development of appropriate values and beliefs.

Objectives:

  • Encourage children and young people to participate in a wide range of activities appropriate to their age and stage of development
  • Assist children and young people in their personal and social development to
  • Encourage growth in self-esteem and confidence and help them learn from their experiences and cope with positive and negative feedbacks critical to their wellbeing
  • Always Promoting the welfare and safety of children and young people, especially the disadvantaged and to unearth the talents and skills of individuals for self-development.
